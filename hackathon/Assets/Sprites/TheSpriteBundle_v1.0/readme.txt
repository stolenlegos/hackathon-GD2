Aekiro - The Sprite Bundle


Included files are PNGs and Source files so you can easily change colors and export to different sizes.
The source files are made in Adobe Illustrator but can be opened  in Inkscape or Affinity Designer.

- Weapons
- Projectiles
- Chains & Ropes
- Crates
- Fences & logs
- Flora (Trees, plants, flowers, etc)
- Ladders
- Potteries
- Planets
- Clouds
- Rocks
- Barrels
